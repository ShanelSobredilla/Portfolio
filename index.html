Hi! I am Shanel. A 19 year old Filipina Designer based in Shanghai, China.

My Design Philosophy
Growing up in a house of designers, I was fortunate enough to appreciate and see the work that goes into creating. I believe that a good design is successful when it translates to people through its simplicity. It may be challenging to create a simple design but when the intended message gets through, there is a sense of purpose for me as a designer. Design is created for people, therefore I put importance on the human aspect that goes into my designs.
My motivation as a designer is to help provide an answer to their problems with the privilege of being able to tell a story or deliver what they need. It is important for me to have a good work ethic so I always make sure to stay focused on each project and manage them well. One thing I value the most about design is the never-ending journey to learn and collaborate. The experience and knowledge I get to pick up from designers is what continues to inspire me in creating.
